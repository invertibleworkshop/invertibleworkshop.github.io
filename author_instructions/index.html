Camera-Ready Instructions

To submit the final camera-ready version of your workshop paper, follow these instructions:
  • Deadline for the camera-ready submission: July 16 23h59 AoE.
  • Make sure to use our style file. This is the same as the style file used for paper submission.
  • In the main .tex file replace "\usepackage{innf2021}” with "\usepackage[accepted]{innf2021}” to submit the camera-ready version.
  • Use a single pdf that contains both the main paper and the appendix. Use a separate .zip file for additional supplementary materials (e.g. code).
  • Submit your camera ready on OpenReview by clicking the “Revision” button next to the author list on the abstract page.

Spotlights & Contributed Talks

We have selected 12 papers for spotlight presentation and 2 papers for contributed talk, which will be pre-recorded and live-streamed during the workshop. The goal of these videos is for you to summarize and highlight the contribution and novelty of your work. The spotlights will be 5-minute long, and the contributed talks will be 25-minute long followed by a 5-minute live Q&A session (the authors of contributed talks will be invited to a zoom room for the Q&A).

If you are an author whose paper has been selected for the spotlight / contributed presentation, and have not received an email that contains the recording link & instructions by June 18, please contact us as soon as possible.
